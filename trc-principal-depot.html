TRC PRINCIPAL DEPOT

CLIENT

Toowoomba Regional Council

 

DURATION

8 Months

 

VALUE

$2.6 M

 

LOCATION

Toowoomba, Queensland

 

Construction of a new principal depot for Toowoomba Regional Council.

Simultaneous delivery of multiple buildings across a 28 Ha Greenfield site, including a central operations building, three workshops, a central stores building, a nursery building and a SES depot.

 

A massive undertaking of in ground water and drainage infrastructure, including fire mains, rain water, potable water mains, sewer and stormwater.

 

A 130L/S dual fire pump booster system, including 540Kl of fire water storage.

 

This project had a large focus on water and energy efficiency, which included a huge rainwater reuse tank and water efficient fixtures and fittings which were installed in all buildings.

 

This project was delivered in an eight month period.
